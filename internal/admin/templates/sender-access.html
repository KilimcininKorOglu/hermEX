Sender allow/block

Rules override spam scoring at delivery: an allowlisted sender skips score-based junking (a forged sender that hard-fails DMARC under an enforcing policy is still junked), a blocklisted sender is always filed to Junk. A pattern is an email address or a bare domain; an exact address beats a domain. Changes apply within about a minute, no restart.
